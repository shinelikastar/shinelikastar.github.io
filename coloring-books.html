coloring books

I worked on Coloring Books, a project to see the colors of literature with three other students at Hack@Brown. Over the course of eight hours, we designed and developed the site, during which I was primarily responsible for calculating the colors in different works of literature. I developed a Python script using word2vec to generate the frequency of colors in each story. I also trained a word2vec model on each story to find the nearest neighbors of each color.
